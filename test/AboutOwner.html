AboutOrlando Holwerda

I am in love with being Fascinated.

I stand in awe of human beings, esthetics, literature, sound, and exploration; and I use that a lot in what I do.


My name is Orlando Holwerda, and I brings stories to life through the visual arts. My primary tool is my camera, and I seek creative actualization through photography and cinematography. I predominantly produce, direct, and edit my own photo shoots and motion picture projects; which is why some of my friends call me "Orland Zoom". When I am in a tough situation or have trouble taking responsibility for something I usually say, "Don't ask me, I'am just the camera guy".


Beauty is easy to see, but hard to explain why.

Photography has been my window of opportunity to adapt and grow with my work. It has inspired me to transition into motion pictures, and to blossom; which reawakens my appreciation for that which is not as still, but fluid and moving.


The challenge of implementing design in my social media presentation also highlights my front-end HTML5, CSS, and Javascript code development.

No project is an island in and of itself.

I have discovered that production and distribution often go hand in hand on the indie level, which also involves discovering, contracting, and promoting artists in collaborative portfolio projects.


My most recent projects involve discovering singers and bands, signing them on to a song single artist agreement with royalty rights, making their music video, packaging them; then helping to distribute and promote them on social media.

The more LA I have, the more I laugh!

I currently live in the District Core of Downtown Los Angeles, where many talented and aspiring design creatives, directors of photography, producers, actors, muscicians and performers with like-minded interests tend to network and congregate in coffe shops and loft studios. I also enjoy the company of film students from USC frequenting the Art and Fashion Districts adjacent to the District Core.


I do have a day job, because the entertainment industry decides on when and where. My favorite ice cream is peanut butter cup, I can yawn in my sleep, creepy crawlies scare me, the bible is my rock, and I love gummy bears.
